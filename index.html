Our Backyard, One Day at a Time

This is our backyard story, told with photos, real backyard sounds, and narration.

Start the Story

Part 1

Text Narrative

In the morning, our backyard is usually super quiet. The grass is still damp, and little sounds stand out way more.

As the day goes on, everything changes a bit. The light moves across the fence, the trees look different, and normal stuff starts feeling important.

By evening, this is where our family hangs out and slows down. We notice details we usually ignore. That was the point of this project: show that a place we see every day can still feel new.
